About
Everyone learns at their own pace and having study material at your time of learning is atmost important. When your papers start piling up and your class notes get disorganized, your college grades can suffer. There's a reason smart students are hyper organized. They use their time studying rather than spending it on finding their class notes.

The pandemic we are going through, is once in a lifetime event. Everything was shut off suddenly , students in hostels were rushed back home leaving their notes , now they miss their notes!! We help you store your notes at one place!

We are getting in touch with Universities to upload their materials of each subject of each year they have in advance, so no one ever lacks learning.
